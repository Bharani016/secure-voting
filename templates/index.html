The Protocol

A secure flow from registration to result.

1

Identity Verification

Voters verify ID via biometric/passport to generate a cryptographic key.

2

Contract Access

The verified address is granted permission to interact with the election smart contract.

3

Cast Encrypted Vote

Votes are encrypted and broadcast as transactions to the decentralized network.

4

Immutable Result

Results are tallied and permanently etched onto the blockchain.
